E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Project Overview

Objective: To drive profitability by analyzing sales trends, product performance, and customer purchasing behavior.

Role: Power BI Developer

Data Processing (ETL)

The dataset consisted of 50,000+ sales records. In Power Query, I:

  • Split the "Customer Address" column to analyze sales by State and City.
  • Created a "Total Cost" custom column (Quantity * Unit Cost) to calculate profit margins.
  • Filtered out returned orders to ensure accurate revenue reporting.

Technical Skills (DAX)

Key sales metrics calculated:

1. Profit Margin %

Profit Margin % = DIVIDE([Total Profit], [Total Revenue], 0)

2. Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth

YoY Sales = DIVIDE([Total Sales] - [Sales Last Year], [Sales Last Year])

3. Average Order Value (AOV)

AOV = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], DISTINCTCOUNT('Sales'[OrderID]))

Insights

  • Insight 1: Electronics have the highest revenue but the lowest profit margin (12%), whereas Accessories have a 45% margin.
  • Insight 2: Q4 sales account for 40% of annual revenue, driven largely by holiday promotions in November.
