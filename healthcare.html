Healthcare Metrics Analysis

Project Overview

Objective: To monitor hospital operational efficiency, tracking patient wait times and resource utilization.

Role: Power BI Developer

Data Processing (ETL)

The dataset included patient admission records and staffing logs. In Power Query, I:

  • Calculated "Wait Time" by subtracting arrival time from consultation time.
  • Grouped data by department to identify bottlenecks.
  • Cleaned inconsistent entries in the "Doctor Specialty" column.

Technical Skills (DAX)

Key metrics calculated:

1. Average Wait Time

Avg Wait Time = AVERAGE('Visits'[WaitTimeMinutes])

2. Patient Satisfaction Score (NPS)

NPS = (CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Surveys'), 'Surveys'[Score] >= 9) - CALCULATE(COUNTROWS('Surveys'), 'Surveys'[Score] <= 6)) / COUNTROWS('Surveys') * 100

Insights & Impact

  • Insight 1: The Emergency Department has the highest average wait time (45 mins) during weekends.
  • Insight 2: Patient satisfaction drops by 20% when wait times exceed 30 minutes.
